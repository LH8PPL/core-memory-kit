The kit is intentionally a **niche, focused project** — the goal is to "be the best at what it does, mostly for me and maybe for somebody who will use it." The user accepts being on "the edge" but rejects competing with generic tools (like graphify). Excellence in the domain outweighs feature coverage.

**Why:** This is a deliberate philosophy that frames all scope and prioritization decisions. Niche depth is the strategy, not a limitation to overcome.

**How to apply:** When deciding what to build next, ask whether it deepens the kit's core (memory + AI experience) or diversifies into adjacent niches. Lean into the first; say no to feature sprawl chasing graphify-parity.
