Session spent ~4.5M subagent tokens. The 79-system enumeration (29 README-level passive triages) yielded ~60% of spend for ~90% of insight. Conversational layer (user corrections, thinking together) had outstanding insight-per-token ratio.

**Why:** Enumeration-for-completeness is seductive but low-ROI. The real work lived in conversational thinking and user-supplied corrections.

**How to apply:** Next research sprint: fan out aggressively for *reading*, stay conversational for *thinking*. Avoid checklist-driven enumeration; prefer curated reading + discussion loop.
