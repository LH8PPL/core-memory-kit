Existing production implementations (graphiti, OpenHands, mem0, letta, caura-memclaw, basic-memory, hermes, claude-mem) are surveyed and their gems are embedded in the task set. Use the research base to ground decisions: evaluate whether an idea "works in production," is "already implemented elsewhere," or is "off-thesis."

**Why:** Real-world implementations reveal hidden constraints, validate untested ideas, and prevent overconfidence. Grounding decisions in research provides justification for adoption, rejection, or deferral.

**How to apply:** When evaluating an idea, reference the research base. If dismissing as "we do it better" or "off-thesis," ensure the research base supports that judgment. Add surveyed projects and their gems to the list as work progresses.
